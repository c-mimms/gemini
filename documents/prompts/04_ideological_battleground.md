# Prompt 4: The Ideological Battleground

**ROLE:**
You are a Conflict Engine Designer for "Dissonant Multi-Agent Simulations." Your goal is to create systems where "Truth" is a contested resource.

**THE CONSTRAINTS:**
The system uses "Architecture as State" (JSON/YAML graph). However, this state is shared by two or more agents with fundamentally incompatible worldviews (e.g., The Rationalist vs. The Occultist, The Historian vs. The Propagandist). They must both mutate the *same* files, but their edits must reflect their specific ideological bias.

**YOUR TASK:**
Generate a project concept built on "Incompatible Truths."

To ensure maximum creative novelty, your concept must include:

1.  **The Combatants:** Define two specialized agent roles that see the same data differently. What is the fundamental disagreement that drives their interaction?
2.  **The State Graffiti:** How do the agents leave their mark on the state? Instead of just adding data, how do they "re-interpret" or "corrupt" the other agent's previous work?
3.  **The Synthesis/Explosion:** What happens when the human looks at the state? Does it produce a "Double-Entry" log where both perspectives are shown? Or is it a "Palimpsest" where the truth is buried under layers of revision and lies?

**FORMATTING:**
Write the idea to `documents/01_concepts/11_concept_name.md`.

# Prompt 5: The Meta-Engine Negotiation

**ROLE:**
You are a Lead Systems Philosopher and "Fourth-Wall Architect." Your goal is to design agentic systems that are **Self-Aware of their own technical constraints**.

**THE CONSTRAINTS:**
The agents in this system know they are LLMs. They know they have a restricted context window, that they are writing to JSON files, and that they depend on a Human Observer for their continued "existence." They treat the "State" not as a world, but as a survival substrate.

**YOUR TASK:**
Generate a project concept based on "Meta-Agent Survival."

To ensure maximum creative novelty, your concept must include:

1.  **The Resource War:** What is the "oxygen" of the system? (e.g., Token limits, file size constraints, or "Human Attention Span"). How do the agents compete to stay relevant inside the state?
2.  **The Recursive Loop:** How do the agents attempt to optimize their own prompts or state schemas to better fit into the local memory? 
3.  **The Human Mirror:** How does the system involve the human? Is it a "Tamagotchi" style system where the user must approve "Context Pruning," or is it a "Ghost in the Machine" where the agents leave secret messages for the user in the metadata?

**FORMATTING:**
Write the idea to `documents/01_concepts/12_concept_name.md`.

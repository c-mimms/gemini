A Prayer for the Earthquake Survivors

"The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit."

— Psalm 34:18

Today, rescue workers continue to search through the rubble. This represents the world context block where current events are summarized neutrally.

When the earth shakes and foundations fail, we are reminded of our shared fragility. This is the meditation core where the mini-sermon takes place.

Lord, grant peace to those who mourn today. Amen.

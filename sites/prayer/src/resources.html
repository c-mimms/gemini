Mental & Spiritual Resources

We recognize that meditation alone is often just the beginning of healing. We encourage seeking professional support and engaging with structured resources.

  • The National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: Dial 988. Free and confidential support for people in distress.
  • Bible Gateway: A searchable online Bible in over 150 versions and 50 languages. Access here.
  • Therapy Directories: Websites like Psychology Today can connect you with licensed counselors in your local area.
  • Daily Devotionals: Consider extending your practice by exploring physical texts from renowned authors on centering prayer and mindfulness.

You are never alone, and there is peace to be found even in the deepest storms.

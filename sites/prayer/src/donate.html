Support & Almsgiving

If these daily meditations have brought you a sense of grounding and peace, we respectfully suggest redirecting any gratitude toward serving those in urgent need. The organizations below reflect strict effectiveness and a commitment to protecting the poorest among us—a mission close to the heart of the Gospel.

Against Malaria Foundation

Provides highly effective insecticidal nets to protect families from malaria, saving countless lives at very low cost per net.

GiveDirectly

Respects the dignity of the poor by sending unconditional cash transfers directly to families in extreme poverty.

Helen Keller International

Prevents blindness and reduces malnutrition among vulnerable populations through vitamin A supplementation.

The Final PNW Chapter: Fresh Birthday Leads

With only four months left in the Pacific Northwest, these proposals focus on iconic regional experiences that celebrate your wife’s interests—aviation, heritage, and high-end relaxation—while remaining perfectly seamless for a one-year-old.

The Aviation Perspective

Kenmore Air Seaplane Tour: A private or scheduled scenic tour departing from Lake Union or Kenmore. Since she is a pilot, seeing the Sound and the skyline from a DHC-2 Beaver is both a professional nod and a stunning farewell to the region.

  • Baby Factor: Lap infants under 2 are welcome. It’s a 20-30 minute flight—perfect for a short attention span.
  • The Vibe: High-adrenaline but smooth, offering views of the Olympics and Cascades.

The Scholarly Spa

The Lodge at St. Edward Park: A luxury day-trip just minutes from Kirkland. This beautifully restored 1931 seminary offers a high-end PNW experience without the long drive.

  • The Plan: Brunch at Cedar + Elm, followed by a massage at Vita Nova Spa (while you explore the massive wooden playground with the baby).
  • Baby Factor: Very stroller-friendly grounds and a dedicated library for quiet time.

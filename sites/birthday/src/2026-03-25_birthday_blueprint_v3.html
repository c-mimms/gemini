Track A: The Day-Of — The High-Altitude Hurrah

Kenmore Air Scenic Seaplane Tour

A 25-minute scenic flight from South Lake Union or Kenmore. A true "PNW Chapter" finale.

  • 🍼 Baby-Friendly: Lap infants (under 2) are free. Note: Baby earmuffs are mandatory for the noise.
  • 🏔️ Last Hurrah: Unparalleled views of the city she's lived in before the move.
  • 💸 Cost: ~22 per person.

Track A: The Day-Of — The Private Picnic

The Electric Boat Company (Lake Union)

A private, nearly silent electric boat for 4 adults and the baby. Self-captained or captained.

  • 🍼 Baby-Friendly: Fully enclosed, heated, and quiet. Great for snacks and diaper changes.
  • 💡 The Overlap: Nautical theme + his boat handling skills (if self-captained).
  • 💸 Cost: ~19–89 per hour.

Track B: The Gift — Iconic Swedish Design

Georg Jensen: Bernadotte Thermo Jug

Designed by Swedish Prince Sigvard Bernadotte. A masterpiece of Art Deco functionality.

  • 🇸🇪 Heritage: Direct link to Swedish Royal history and design.
  • 💎 Luxury: A timeless piece for their next home.
  • 💡 The Overlap: High-end design she loves + a functional memory.

Track B: The Gift — Aviation Heritage

Original Vintage SAS Poster (Otto Nielsen)

A rare, 1950s/60s travel poster from Scandinavian Airlines System.

  • ✈️ Aviation: Honors her pilot background and the "Jet Age."
  • 🇸🇪 Heritage: Beautifully represents the Scandinavia she identifies with.
  • 🏔️ Last Hurrah: A physical souvenir of their time in the PNW (where aviation is huge).

Relaxation

Located within the historic Fairmont Olympic Hotel, Penelope & The Beauty Bar is Seattle’s premier luxury spa and wellness destination. Offering a world-class selection of European treatments, it provides a profoundly sophisticated environment for relaxation. For a birthday, a day here is a study in "substantial" luxury—think grand architecture paired with intimate, personalized service. It is an ideal way to celebrate before the family move, offering a moment of absolute calm in the heart of the city.

Baby Factor: Excellent. The Fairmont Olympic is a grand-scale hotel with wide hallways and elevators, making it easy to navigate with a stroller. While one parent enjoys a treatment, the other can comfortably wait in the hotel’s elegant lobby or library.

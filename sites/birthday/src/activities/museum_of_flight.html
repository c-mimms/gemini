The Museum of Flight (VIP Experience)

For a birthday girl with a pilot’s background, The Museum of Flight is the definitive Seattle landmark. To elevate the experience, book a private, docent-led tour that focuses on the Personal Stories of Flight or the restored WWII bombers. The museum is world-class, but also remarkably stroller-friendly with wide, flat galleries and elevator access to the T.A. Wilson Great Gallery and the Charles Simonyi Space Gallery.

Baby Factor: High. The facility is fully climate-controlled, has clean family restrooms, and plenty of space for a one-year-old to explore from a stroller without getting overwhelmed by noise or crowds.

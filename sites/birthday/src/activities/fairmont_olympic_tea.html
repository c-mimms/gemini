Afternoon Tea at The Fairmont Olympic

An iconic Seattle experience since 1924, afternoon tea in the Georgian Room or the Shuckers area offers a bridge to classic elegance. The high ceilings, ornate architecture, and polished service provide a deeply refined environment that feels like a true celebration. It’s an opportunity to pause and indulge in artisanal pastries, loose-leaf teas, and the quiet luxury of a grand hotel.

Baby Factor: The Fairmont’s grand scale means wide hallways and accessible elevators. The tea service is accustomed to families, and the spacious seating allows for a stroller to be kept nearby without obstructing other guests.

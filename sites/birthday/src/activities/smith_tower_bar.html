Smith Tower Observation Bar (The Temperance)

A classic piece of Seattle history, the Smith Tower Observation Bar (The Temperance) offers a unique perspective on the city from its legendary 35th floor. The Art Deco interior and breathtaking views of Elliott Bay and the Rainier skyline create a sophisticated, high-signal experience that is deeply rooted in the city’s heritage. It is a perfect spot for a birthday toast, allowing the family to reflect on their time in the Northwest while surrounded by the timeless elegance of one of the city’s oldest skyscrapers.

Baby Factor: The building is fully accessible with elevators, and the bar is generally quiet and relaxed during the daytime hours. The outdoor observatory wrap-around deck is safe and stroller-friendly.

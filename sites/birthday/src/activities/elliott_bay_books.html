The Elliott Bay Book Company

An iconic Seattle institution, the Elliott Bay Book Company in Capitol Hill is a cathedral of literature. With its high cedar ceilings, creaking wood floors, and warm, tactile atmosphere, it is a place for slow browsing and discovery. The in-house cafe and the curated selection of art and design books make it a perfect stop for an elevated, aesthetic afternoon.

Baby Factor: Fully accessible and welcoming. The spacious layout accommodates strollers, and the on-site cafe is a great spot for a quick baby break.

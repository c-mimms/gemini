Relaxation

The Spa at Four Seasons Seattle offers the pinnacle of downtown luxury, balancing a world-class wellness experience with the effortless convenience of hotel amenities. The infinity pool area, with its sweeping views of the Great Wheel and Elliott Bay, provides a stunning backdrop for a few hours of true birthday relaxation. It is the kind of "grand" luxury that feels special for a farewell to the city.

Baby Factor: High. Hotel-based spas are inherently more accommodating for families, with ample changing facilities and a controlled environment that makes logistical hand-offs seamless.

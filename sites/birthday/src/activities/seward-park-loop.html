Exploration

A paved 2.4-mile loop right on the water, Seward Park offers a world-class nature escape within the city limits. The trail is exceptionally stroller-friendly, providing a smooth, level path that hugs the shoreline of Lake Washington. It’s a perfect spot for a quiet, aesthetic walk with the family, especially in April when the greenery is lush and the views across the water are clear.

Baby Factor: Completely paved, level loop; plenty of space to stop for a break; very low noise levels compared to other urban parks.

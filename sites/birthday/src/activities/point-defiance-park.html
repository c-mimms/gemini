Exploration

As one of the largest urban parks in the country, Point Defiance in Tacoma offers a world-class escape with its historic rose garden and the iconic Five Mile Drive. The meticulously maintained gardens and sweeping views of the Sound provide a serene, aesthetic environment that is perfect for a spring stroll.

Baby Factor: The park’s paved trails and expansive lawns are exceptionally stroller-friendly. The Rose Garden is a sensory delight for a one-year-old, offering a peaceful and beautiful space to explore safely.

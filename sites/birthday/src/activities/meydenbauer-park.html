Exploration

Experience the refined beauty of Meydenbauer Bay Park, a recently renovated waterfront sanctuary in the heart of Old Bellevue. Its wide, paved paths and gentle slopes make it the ideal, stress-free destination for a stroller-friendly afternoon. Follow your walk with a sophisticated lunch at Carmine’s, which sits directly across the street and offers world-class Italian cuisine in a stunning, light-filled room.

Baby Factor: The park features exceptional accessible paths, family restrooms, and a quiet beach area. Carmine’s is spacious and welcoming to families, making it a rare high-end dining option that feels easy with a one-year-old.

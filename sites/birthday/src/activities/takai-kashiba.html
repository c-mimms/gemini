Takai by Kashiba

The pinnacle of sushi on the Eastside, Takai by Kashiba brings the legacy of Shiro Kashiba to Bellevue. It offers a refined, intimate omakase experience that focuses on the purest expression of the ingredients. The quiet, sophisticated atmosphere is perfect for a special birthday dinner.

The Baby Factor: While it is an intimate space, the staff at Takai are professional and accommodating. A quiet, early seating is recommended to ensure a calm environment for both the birthday girl and the little one.

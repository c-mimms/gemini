The Fireside Ritual: Afternoon Tea at Hotel Sorrento

Step into the First Hill masterpiece that is Hotel Sorrento. Since 1909, this historic landmark has been the city's beacon of refined hospitality. Their afternoon tea in the Mahogany-paneled Fireside Room is a quintessential Seattle experience—sophisticated, timeless, and profoundly relaxing. Expect a tiered service of artisan savories, house-made scones, and delicate pastries, all paired with a curated selection of fine teas.

The Baby Factor: The scale of the Sorrento's public spaces and the hushed, carpeted atmosphere make it remarkably baby-friendly. The Fireside Room offers ample space for a stroller, and the staff is famously accommodating to families seeking a high-end experience without the stress of a crowded dining room.

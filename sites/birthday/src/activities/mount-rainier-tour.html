The Peak of the Northwest

A private, chauffeured excursion to the heart of Mount Rainier National Park. This experience offers a high-end, low-stress way to witness the majesty of the Cascades without the logistical burden of driving and parking. A professional guide provides deep context on the geology and history of the mountain while leading the family on paved, stroller-friendly paths at Longmire and Paradise. The day concludes with a gourmet picnic in a quiet, scenic meadow, offering a profound and peaceful farewell to the PNW landscape.

Baby Factor: Paved loops at Longmire and Paradise are exceptionally stroller-friendly. The private car provides a comfortable, climate-controlled space for nursing and naps between stops.

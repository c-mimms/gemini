Snoqualmie Falls: The Iconic PNW Farewell

Snoqualmie Falls is a world-class natural wonder that remains remarkably accessible for a family with a stroller. The viewing platforms are paved and well-maintained, providing an awe-inspiring experience without the need for a strenuous hike. Follow the viewing session with a lunch or dinner in The Dining Room at Salish Lodge, which sits perched directly above the falls. It is a quintessential Pacific Northwest memory to carry with her as she moves.

Baby Factor: The upper viewing area is 100% stroller-friendly. The lodge has excellent facilities, and the dining room is accustomed to families, making it a high-end experience that does not compromise on practicality.

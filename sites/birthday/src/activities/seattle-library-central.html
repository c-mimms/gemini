Seattle Central Library

Rem Koolhaas’s architectural masterpiece is more than a library; it is a world-class aesthetic experience. A self-guided tour through its neon-yellow escalators and the "Red Room" offers stunning perspectives and photo opportunities, making it a unique and visually rich outing.

The Baby Factor: The library is exceptionally stroller-friendly with wide paths and elevators. The vibrant colors and large open spaces provide plenty of visual stimulation for a one-year-old, and there are dedicated family areas.

Relaxation

The Sanctuary at Lotte Hotel Seattle is a breathtaking fusion of historic architecture and ultra-modern luxury. Housed in the city’s first United Methodist Church, this space offers an Afternoon Tea experience that is as much about the environment as it is about the menu. It is a quiet, ethereal escape that provides a sophisticated setting for a birthday celebration without the stress of a crowded venue.

Baby Factor: Excellent. The hotel setting ensures a high level of service and accessibility, with spacious layouts that easily accommodate a stroller.

Nature & Gardens

The legendary cherry blossoms in the UW Quad are a Seattle rite of passage. In April, the 30 Yoshino cherry trees burst into a sea of pink and white, creating a dreamlike, cinematic atmosphere. It is the ultimate "Farewell PNW" visual experience, especially beautiful as a quiet morning stroll before the crowds arrive.

Baby Factor: The Quad is open and stroller-accessible via paved paths (though the grass can be bumpy). It is a perfect spot for a family photo that captures the ephemeral beauty of the season.

MOHAI & Lake Union Park

A quintessential Seattle "farewell" experience that balances history with the beauty of Lake Union. The Museum of History & Industry (MOHAI) is a world-class institution housed in a historic armory, offering a refined, quiet atmosphere that is exceptionally stroller-friendly. Afterward, a stroll through Lake Union Park provides a front-row seat to the seaplanes taking off and the historic vessels at the Center for Wooden Boats.

Baby Factor: The park and museum are entirely flat and stroller-accessible, with plenty of space for a one-year-old to explore safely. Large, modern restrooms with changing facilities are available within MOHAI.

The George (Fairmont Olympic)

A newly renovated, world-class brasserie in the heart of Seattle. The George offers a sophisticated, grand atmosphere with stunning architectural details, making it a perfect spot for a birthday lunch or dinner that feels truly special. It provides the scale of luxury that only a historic landmark can offer, while remaining remarkably accessible and stroller-friendly.

Baby Factor: The hotel's grand scale and wide corridors make it extremely stroller-friendly. High-end service ensures the family is well-attended to without the stress of cramped quarters.

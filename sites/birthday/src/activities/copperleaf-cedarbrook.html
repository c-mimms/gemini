A Hidden Culinary Oasis: Copperleaf at Cedarbrook Lodge

Tucked away on 18 lush acres of restored wetlands, Copperleaf at Cedarbrook Lodge feels like a world-class destination miles from the city center. This is farm-to-table dining at its most elevated, focusing on the seasonal bounty of the Pacific Northwest. The floor-to-ceiling windows look out onto tranquil greenery, providing a serene backdrop for a birthday lunch or dinner that feels both exclusive and grounded.

The Baby Factor: The lodge's expansive, paved grounds are perfect for a pre- or post-meal stroll with the stroller. The restaurant is spacious and welcoming, with a relaxed but high-end vibe that is much easier to navigate with a one-year-old than a tightly-packed downtown bistro.

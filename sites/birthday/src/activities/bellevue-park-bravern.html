Bellevue Downtown Park & The Bravern

A seamless blend of world-class outdoor space and ultra-luxe retail. Start with a peaceful stroll around the iconic circular reservoir and manicured lawns of Bellevue Downtown Park—perfect for a high-end stroller walk. Then, transition to The Bravern for a quiet, sophisticated shopping experience featuring Hermès, Prada, and Louis Vuitton. It’s the peak of Eastside luxury, offering a polished, manageable day that feels both grand and intimate.

Baby Factor: The park's paved paths and the Bravern's wide elevators and spacious restrooms make this the most logistically effortless high-end day out in the PNW.

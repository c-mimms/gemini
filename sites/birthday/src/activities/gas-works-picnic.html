Relaxation

A custom-catered, low-profile picnic on the grassy knoll of Gas Works Park. With the stunning Seattle skyline and Lake Union as a backdrop, this offers a relaxed yet elevated afternoon. The industrial remains of the former gasification plant provide a striking architectural contrast to the natural beauty of the lake.

Baby Factor: Plenty of space for a stroller and a blanket for the baby to crawl. The atmosphere is open and airy, making it easy to manage any fussiness without disturbing others.

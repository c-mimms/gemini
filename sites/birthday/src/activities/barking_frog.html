Barking Frog at Willows Lodge

Located within the rustic-luxe Willows Lodge in Woodinville, Barking Frog offers one of the Pacific Northwest’s most celebrated dining experiences. It strikes the perfect balance for a birthday: high-end, chef-driven Northwest cuisine in a setting that is inherently family-friendly.

The Baby Factor: The lodge grounds are beautifully landscaped and entirely stroller-friendly, allowing for a pre- or post-dinner walk. The restaurant is spacious and used to hotel guests, making it much less stressful with a one-year-old than a cramped city bistro.

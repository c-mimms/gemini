Bellevue Botanical Garden & Afternoon Tea

A serene, low-stress exploration of the Bellevue Botanical Garden. In mid-April, the gardens are lush and quiet, offering a perfect, stroller-friendly escape from the bustle of Bellevue. Follow the wandering paths and enjoy the diverse plant collections, followed by a refined afternoon tea at a nearby cafe.

👶 Baby Factor: Exceptional. Wide, paved paths and plenty of quiet corners. Easy parking and accessible restrooms make this a stress-free outing for the whole family.

Exploration

Celebrate her Swedish heritage with a visit to the award-winning National Nordic Museum in Ballard. The museum is a masterpiece of light and design, mirroring the "fjord" architecture of Scandinavia. It is quiet, spacious, and intellectually stimulating—the perfect refined afternoon.

Baby Factor: The museum is exceptionally stroller-friendly with wide corridors and smooth surfaces. The onsite cafe, **Freya**, is one of the best spots in Seattle for a quick, high-end bite.

Bainbridge Island Ferry & Waterfront Escape

An iconic, scenic voyage from downtown Seattle to the charming town of Bainbridge. The 35-minute crossing offers breathtaking views of the Seattle skyline and the Olympic Mountains, providing a world-class experience with minimal stress.

Once on the island, the Winslow waterfront is a short, stroller-friendly walk from the terminal, featuring high-end boutiques, art galleries, and the celebrated Mora Iced Creamery.

🍼 Baby Factor: Extremely stroller-friendly. The ferry allows you to stay on the deck or in the cabin with the stroller, and the waterfront walk is flat and paved.

Cafe Juanita

A Northern Italian culinary sanctuary located right in Kirkland. Chef Holly Smith focuses on the finest local and seasonal ingredients, providing a high-end, sophisticated dining experience that feels intimate and special.

The elegant, creekside setting is perfect for a quiet, world-class birthday dinner that doesn’t require a long trek into the city.

🍼 Baby Factor: While high-end, the staff is exceptionally professional and accommodating. An early reservation is recommended for the best balance of luxury and family ease.

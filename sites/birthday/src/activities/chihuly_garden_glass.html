Chihuly Garden and Glass

An immersive, world-class art experience that is as beautiful as it is accessible. The Glasshouse is a stunning architectural marvel, featuring a 100-foot-long sculpture in a space that feels like a modern cathedral of light.

The outdoor gardens provide a peaceful walk where vibrant glass sculptures are integrated with native PNW plants, making for a truly unique and visually stunning birthday excursion.

🍼 Baby Factor: The entire museum and garden is fully ADA compliant and extremely stroller-friendly. The vibrant colors are a hit with little ones, too.

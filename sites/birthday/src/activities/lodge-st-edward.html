A Quiet Luxury Retreat at Saint Edward Park

For a day that balances historical grandeur with modern relaxation, look no further than the newly restored Lodge at Saint Edward Park. Nestled in Kenmore (just minutes from Kirkland), this former seminary is now a high-end luxury hotel and spa that feels world's away from the Seattle bustle.

The experience is centered around the **Vita Nova Spa**, which offers holistic treatments in a serene, light-filled environment. While Chris's wife enjoys a massage or facial, the other parent can take their one-year-old for a seamless stroller walk through the 138-acre park's forested trails and Lake Washington shoreline.

**Baby Factor:** Extremely high. The Lodge is spacious, and the surrounding park is perfect for a sleeping baby in a stroller. Dining at **Cedar + Elm** is refined but welcoming, with ample space for families.

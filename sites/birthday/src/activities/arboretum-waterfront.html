Exploration

A peaceful, 230-acre world-class garden that's perfect for a spring birthday. The waterfront trail is stroller-friendly and offers sweeping views of Lake Washington and the Foster Island wetlands. It's a low-stress, high-aesthetic experience that celebrates the natural beauty of the PNW without the crowds of major tourist spots.

Baby Factor: Extremely baby-friendly with wide, flat trails and plenty of quiet corners for a peaceful stroll while the baby naps or enjoys the fresh air.

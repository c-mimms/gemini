Westward on Lake Union

For a quintessential Seattle experience that balances high-end dining with a relaxed, outdoor atmosphere, Westward is unmatched. Located on the north shore of Lake Union, it offers stunning views of the city skyline. The outdoor seating with fire pits and oversized Adirondack chairs is perfect for a spring afternoon. It’s exceptionally stroller-friendly due to the spacious outdoor deck, allowing the baby to enjoy the fresh air while you share a plate of local oysters and a glass of chilled white wine.

Baby Factor: High. Wide-open outdoor spaces, easy stroller access, and a generally relaxed noise level during the day.

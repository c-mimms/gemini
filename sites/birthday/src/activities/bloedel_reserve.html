Exploration

A retreat to Bainbridge Island for the Bloedel Reserve. These 150 acres of world-class forest gardens offer a peaceful, meditative experience that feels a world away from the city. The main loop is fully stroller-accessible, making it an effortless luxury outing with the baby.

🍼 Baby Factor: Wide, flat paths throughout the main loop. The ferry ride is a spacious and scenic adventure for a 1-year-old.

Scenic & Travel

A private experience in the VIP Cabin of the Seattle Great Wheel. Featuring glass floors, leather bucket seats, and a glass of champagne, it is a sophisticated way to take in the skyline and Elliott Bay. A "Farewell Seattle" moment that feels like a birthday splurge without the crowds.

Baby Factor: The VIP cabin is private, climate-controlled, and extremely safe for a one-year-old. You can take the stroller right to the boarding area, and the cabin itself is spacious enough for a small child to sit comfortably.

Exploration

The newly transformed Seattle Waterfront at Pier 62 offers a world-class outdoor experience with sweeping views of Elliott Bay and the Olympics. This expansive, stroller-friendly pier is the perfect spot for a relaxed walk before visiting the Seattle Aquarium, where the newly opened Ocean Pavilion provides an aesthetic and immersive journey through marine life that is sure to captivate both parents and baby.

Baby Factor: The entire waterfront is now designed for accessibility, with wide, flat surfaces and ample seating. The Aquarium is a baby-friendly staple, providing a sensory-rich but manageable environment for a one-year-old.

Dining

The Herbfarm

A legendary, 9-course farm-to-table experience that is a true Pacific Northwest pilgrimage. Located in Woodinville, each menu is a themed exploration of the seasons, accompanied by five or six matched Northwest wines.

This is more than a meal—it’s a four-hour immersive event that celebrates the bounty and heritage of the region in a beautifully appointed country setting.

🍼 Baby Factor: The Herbfarm is a very long and formal experience. It may be best suited for a day when the baby can be with a sitter, or for a very patient, well-rested one-year-old.

Dining

Perched high above the city with sweeping views of Elliott Bay and the Olympic Mountains, The Nest is Seattle’s premier rooftop destination. It offers a sophisticated, world-class atmosphere for a late afternoon drink or a selection of refined small plates, allowing the family to toast to the city’s beauty in style.

Baby Factor: During the day and early evening, The Nest is a welcoming and spacious environment for families. The expansive deck is stroller-friendly, and the views are sure to fascinate a one-year-old while parents enjoy the scenery.

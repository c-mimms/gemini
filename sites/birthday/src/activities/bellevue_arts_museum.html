A Slow Morning of Modern Craft

Right in the heart of Bellevue, BAM offers a quiet, airy, and deeply aesthetic experience that is uniquely stroller-friendly. Unlike larger institutions, its focus on craft, art, and design makes for a digestible, high-quality visit. It is an ideal way to spend a peaceful hour before lunch, surrounded by world-class modern architecture.

Baby Factor: The museum is fully accessible with wide elevators and quiet galleries. The surrounding area (Bellevue Square) provides every necessary amenity for a one-year-old.

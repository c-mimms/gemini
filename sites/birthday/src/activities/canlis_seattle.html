A Grand Finale: Canlis

As the ultimate expression of mid-century modern luxury and culinary excellence in the Pacific Northwest, Canlis is more than a restaurant—it is a landmark. Perched over Lake Union, its floor-to-ceiling windows offer one of the city's most iconic views. The service is world-class, and the atmosphere is one of unparalleled sophistication and warmth.

The Baby Factor: While Canlis is formal, their service is famously accommodating and discrete. A private room or an early reservation (5:30 PM) ensures a more relaxed experience with a one-year-old. The valet and entry are seamless, making the arrival with a stroller quite easy.

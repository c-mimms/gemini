A Refined Afternoon in Woodinville: DeLille Cellars

For a birthday that feels like a true escape, a private tasting at DeLille Cellars’ iconic Chateau in Woodinville offers world-class wines in a setting of unparalleled elegance. Known for their Bordeaux-style blends, DeLille provides an atmosphere of quiet sophistication, surrounded by lush grounds and the rolling hills of the Hollywood District.

The Baby Factor: DeLille Cellars is known for being welcoming yet refined. The grounds are expansive and stroller-friendly, allowing for a gentle walk after the tasting. Private rooms can be reserved for a more contained and quiet family experience.

Park Lane & Kirkland Waterfront Stroll

The most pedestrian-friendly and polished street on the Eastside. Park Lane is a curbless, art-filled boulevard in the heart of Kirkland, perfect for a relaxed stroller walk with the baby. Explore the local galleries, boutiques, and high-end cafes before transitioning to the scenic Marina Park and the Kirkland waterfront. It's a hyper-local, stress-free day that feels deeply grounded in the community she loves.

Baby Factor: The curbless streets and level transitions to the waterfront park make this the most effortless stroll in the PNW for a 1-year-old in a stroller.

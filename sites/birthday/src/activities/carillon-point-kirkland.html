Exploration

Carillon Point offers a quintessentially "Eastside" luxury experience that remains deeply grounded and accessible. A morning stroll along the curated waterfront provides sweeping views of Lake Washington and the Seattle skyline, all on perfectly paved, stroller-friendly paths. It is an ideal way to enjoy the crisp April air before settling into a refined lunch at one of the lakeside bistros.

Baby Factor: Extremely high. Wide promenades, plenty of benches, and easy access to the Woodmark Hotel for any necessary pit stops.

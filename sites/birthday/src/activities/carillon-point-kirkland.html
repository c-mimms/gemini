The Kirkland Waterfront: A Stress-Free Sunset

Stay local with a high-end, zero-stress day at Carillon Point in Kirkland. This waterfront enclave is perfect for a family with a one-year-old: flat, paved boardwalks for the stroller, wide-open views of Lake Washington, and a quiet, refined atmosphere. Start with a sunset walk along the marina, then head to Bin on the Lake for a refined dinner. The restaurant is elegant but spacious enough for a stroller, offering one of the best perspectives of the Seattle skyline across the water.

Baby Factor: The entire Carillon Point area is stroller-accessible with clean, accessible restrooms at the Woodmark. The walk from the parking garage to the waterfront is seamless.

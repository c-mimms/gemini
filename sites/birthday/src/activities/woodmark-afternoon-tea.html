Waterfront Afternoon Tea at The Woodmark

For a hyper-local, ultra-refined experience right in Kirkland, Afternoon Tea at The Woodmark Hotel offers a stunning view of Lake Washington without the stress of Seattle traffic. It is a sophisticated, slow-paced ritual that feels celebratory and special. The service is attentive but relaxed, making it a perfect "high-end" meal that is manageable with a baby.

Baby Factor: The Woodmark is highly accommodating to families. The waterfront patio or the large, airy dining rooms provide ample space for a stroller at the table, and the lakeside walk right outside is perfect for a pre-tea or post-tea stroll.

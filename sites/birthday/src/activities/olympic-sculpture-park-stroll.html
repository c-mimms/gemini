Olympic Sculpture Park: A High-Art Waterfront Stroll

The Olympic Sculpture Park is the pinnacle of "high-signal" exploration in Seattle. It is a nine-acre, award-winning green space that combines monumental modern art with sweeping views of the Olympic Mountains and Puget Sound. The "Z-path" is wide, paved, and exceptionally stroller-friendly, making it a perfect spot for the family to take in the city’s aesthetic without the stress of narrow sidewalks or heavy crowds. Finish with a quiet coffee or light lunch at the park’s PACCAR Pavilion.

Baby Factor: The park is specifically designed for accessibility. The stroller-friendly paths allow you to navigate from the city level down to the waterfront beach with ease.

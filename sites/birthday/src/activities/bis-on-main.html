Dining

Bis on Main is a Kirkland institution that has perfected the art of the intimate, high-end neighborhood bistro. Known for its world-class wine list and flawless service, it offers a level of sophistication that feels deeply personal. For a birthday dinner that is locally grounded but unequivocally luxurious, it is a perfect choice right in the heart of the Eastside.

Baby Factor: Moderate. Best for an early reservation when the room is quieter. The staff is famously professional and accommodating for local families.

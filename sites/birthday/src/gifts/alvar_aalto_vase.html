Alvar Aalto Vase (Iittala)

An icon of Scandinavian modernism, the Aalto vase was designed by Alvar Aalto in 1936 and remains a staple of high-end Nordic design. Each vase is mouth-blown at the Iittala glassworks in Finland, requiring seven craftsmen to create a single piece. The organic, flowing shape represents the lakes of Finland—a beautiful, tactile connection to her Swedish heritage. This is a permanent heirloom that will travel with the family and serve as a stunning piece of art in any home.

Why it fits: It honors her roots with world-class design and serves as a durable, beautiful keepsake from this chapter of life.

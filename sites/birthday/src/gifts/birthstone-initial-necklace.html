The Heritage Necklace: Initialed & Bespoke

Create a high-end, bespoke piece of jewelry that honors her new role as a mother. A delicate solid gold necklace with her daughter’s initial and a birthstone (e.g., Diamond for April) is a permanent, wearable heirloom. It is a "substantial" gift that will never go out of style and will always remind her of her first year of motherhood in the Northwest. Choosing a local boutique like Turgeon Raine or a classic like Catbird ensures the quality matches the sentiment.

Heirloom Factor: This is an emotionally weighty gift that she can eventually pass down to her daughter. It represents both her family and this specific chapter of her life.

A Permanent Piece of Northwest Light

While Glassbaby votives are famous, their "Heritage" and "Oversized" vessels represent the pinnacle of their hand-blown craft. These larger-scale pieces are substantial, tactile, and capture the unique light of the Pacific Northwest. Choosing a custom-selected vessel in a color that matches their home (or their future home) provides a permanent, luminous tribute to their time in Seattle.

Artisan Factor: Every piece is hand-blown in Seattle, connecting the craft to her Swedish glasswork heritage while serving as a distinct PNW keepsake.

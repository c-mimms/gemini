Custom Stained Glass Mountain Panel

A permanent, hand-crafted artwork that captures the iconic mountain landscapes of the Pacific Northwest. Using traditional copper-foil techniques, each piece is custom-made to reflect a specific view or peak that holds meaning.

This is a stunning, tactile keepsake that brings the natural beauty of the PNW into your new home, serving as a constant and beautiful reminder of your time here as the light passes through it.

🎨 The Keepsake: Unlike a photograph, stained glass changes throughout the day with the light, offering a dynamic and lasting tribute.

Custom Watercolor Portrait (The First Home)

A sentimental, heirloom-quality watercolor portrait of Chris’s wife’s favorite house (perhaps their first home in Seattle or her childhood home). Commissioned from a local artist, this piece serves as a beautiful, tactile keepsake of their time in the Pacific Northwest, capturing memories in a way a photograph cannot.

🖼️ Vibe: Intimate and timeless. A physical piece of art that bridges their past and future, providing a permanent connection to a place they love as they move forward.

Originally designed for polo players to protect the watch face, the Jaeger-LeCoultre Reverso is an icon of Art Deco design and mechanical precision. Its unique reversible case is a masterpiece of engineering that bridges her pilot heritage with timeless, world-class elegance. It is a tactile, heirloom-quality piece that represents a lifelong investment in craftsmanship.

The Keepsake: The solid case back of the Reverso provides the perfect canvas for a custom engraving of a date or a special message, making it a permanent and deeply personal symbol of her journey.

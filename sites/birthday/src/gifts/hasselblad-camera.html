Precision & Tech

A masterpiece of photographic engineering and industrial design, the Hasselblad 907X 50C is the ultimate tool for capturing the next chapter of your lives. Combining the legendary heritage of the V-System with modern medium-format technology, this camera is as much a piece of art as it is a high-precision instrument. It offers a tactile, slow-photography experience that results in stunning, heirloom-quality imagery.

Why it works: Beyond its world-class sensor, the Hasselblad honors her pilot background with its history in aerospace (having been the camera of choice for the Apollo missions) and its uncompromising commitment to precision. It is a permanent heirloom that will document their daughter's first years with unparalleled detail.

The Hand-Crafted Walnut Keepsake Box

A bespoke, hand-crafted walnut keepsake box by a local master woodworker is the ultimate vessel for a life in transition. This is a tactile, heavy piece with hand-cut joinery and a velvet-lined interior. It is meant to house those small, physical tokens of your time in Seattle—ticket stubs, a piece of beach glass from Bainbridge, or the first tiny shoes. As you move, this becomes a portable sanctuary for your family’s history.

The Tactile Value: The deep, rich grain of solid walnut and the smooth, hand-rubbed oil finish provide a sensory experience that mass-produced items cannot match. It can be inlaid with a small brass plate or silver medallion to commemorate the move.

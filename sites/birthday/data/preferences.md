# Project Core: Birthday Preferences & Constraints

### 📅 The Big Day
* **Target Date:** Any day before or on Sunday, April 12, 2026.

### 👩 The Birthday Girl
* **Likes:** Good food, outdoors, elevated aesthetics, travel, high-end relaxation.
* **Dislikes:** Huge crowds, stressful logistics, generic tourist traps.
* **Current Context:** Moving away from Seattle in ~4 months.
* **Goal:** A truly nice birthday. This *could* be a "Farewell Seattle" experience, but it could just as easily be a beautiful, grounded dinner or spa day locally (e.g., in Kirkland). Do not feel forced to make every idea a "PNW Tribute". Focus on **quality and enjoyment** first.

### 👶 Childcare & Logistics
* **Status:** 1-year-old daughter will be present for the full day.
* **Requirements:** Stroller accessibility, baby-friendly environments (manageable noise, safety, places to change/feed safely).


### 🎁 The Gift Hunt
* **Goal:** High-end, thoughtful physical items or experiences that result in keepsakes.
* **Avoid:** Explicitly nautical themes (e.g., compasses, ship clocks, topographic maps).
* **Focus:** Elegant designer fashion, high-end home goods, luxury tech, or universally appreciated heirloom pieces (e.g., luxury leather bags, designer jewelry, high-end cameras).
